Neuralink AI — About

Neuralink AI is a deep learning system for classifying brain MRI scans into four diagnostic categories. Built for educational and research demonstration purposes. Not intended for clinical use.

Tumour Classes

Class Description Severity
Glioma
Tumour originating from glial cells of the brain or spine.
High
Meningioma
Tumour arising from the meninges, the protective membranes of the CNS.
Medium
Pituitary
Tumour located in or near the pituitary gland at the base of the brain.
Medium
No Tumour
No tumour detected in the MRI scan.
None

Model Architecture

NETWORK SPECIFICATIONS
base_model MobileNetV2
pretrain_weights ImageNet
input_shape 224 × 224 × 3 (RGB)
output_classes 4
output_activation softmax
augmentation Rotation, shifts, zoom, flips, brightness
framework TensorFlow / Keras 2.x

Training Strategy

TWO-PHASE TRANSFER LEARNING
1
Feature Extraction (Frozen)
MobileNetV2 base layers are frozen. Only the classification head is trained, allowing the model to leverage ImageNet visual features on the MRI domain.
2
Fine-Tuning (Unfrozen)
Upper layers of the base model are unfrozen and retrained with a lower learning rate, adapting the network's high-level representations to the specific characteristics of brain MRI imagery.
